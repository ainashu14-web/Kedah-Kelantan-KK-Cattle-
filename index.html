Bioscience Analytics: Derivatives in Animal Husbandry

Modeling the Growth of Kedah-Kelantan (KK) Cattle in Malaysia

The Biological Problem

In Malaysian beef production, maximizing Feed Conversion Ratio (FCR) is vital. As an Animal Husbandry student, you must identify the Point of Inflection—where the cattle's daily weight gain starts to slow down. Past this point, keeping the animal becomes less profitable as feed costs outweigh the gain in meat.

Data inspired by MARDI (Malaysian Agricultural Research and Development Institute) growth studies for local KK crossbreeds.

The Mathematics

The Derivative $f'(t)$
